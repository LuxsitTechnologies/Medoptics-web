Our CEO

The Year 2019 when Mr. Atif Imran the CEO of MedOptics set his strong footing in Ophthalmic business by establishing his business in Lahore.
In Year 2002 after completion of MBA (Major in Sales and Marketing) started his career in Pharmedic Laboratories as Medical Information Officer where he served more then five years. He learned so many professional sales training and his learning curve improved in every year. He won many awards and distinctions in this organization.
In 2007 He joined a national Ophthalmic company and served there almost 14 years. He joined as Sales Executive but due to his strong commitment and hardworking he left this company as National Sales Manager. He was the first in this company who won the One Million Man Award. Best Sales person Award in many consecutive years and many more. He lead the team of more then 20 persons all across Pakistan. He was responsible to promote ad sell Phaco machines, Laser machines, OCT, and Intra Ocular Lenses.
He attended many national and international ophthalmic conferences in Asia Europe and improve his vision in establishing his business in Ophthalmic industry.
